Supported Regulations

EC 261/2004

European Union

Covers flights departing from or arriving at EU airports on EU-based airlines. Compensation €250–€600.

  • Delays of 3+ hours
  • Flight cancellations
  • Denied boarding
UK261

United Kingdom

Mirrors EC 261/2004 post-Brexit. Applies to UK airports and UK-based carriers. Compensation £220–£520.

  • Same rights as EU regulation
  • UK airports & airlines
  • Post-Brexit protection
US DOT

United States

DOT regulations mandate full ticket refunds and compensation for involuntary bumping on US flights.

  • Denied boarding compensation
  • Full refund rights
  • Domestic & international
